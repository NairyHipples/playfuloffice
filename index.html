The Playful Office philosophy is developed by doctors, scientific researchers, psychologists, interior designers, product developers, artists, architects, philosophers, horticulturists, computer technicians, nerds and programmers with many years of experience.

Playful Office is not some wild guess or some random prediction. This is a well compiled collection of research, observations, interviews and a whole lot of experience. This is happening now, as you are reading this, this is the future.
